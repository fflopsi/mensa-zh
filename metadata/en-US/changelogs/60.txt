New features and settings (some requested by you, thanks!), bugfixes and performance improvements!

New settings to fit your needs:

* Choose between 2 or 3 lines of detail in list overview
* Enable allergen display in list overview
* Choose to automatically show the image of the tapped menu
* Sort or hide menus by types (standard (meat, fish etc.), vegetarian, vegan) [Warning: labeling not done by the app, may be inconsistent or wrong]
* Choose the app language in your system settings (translation contributions appreciated!)
* Clear the cached menus in one tap

What's new otherwise:

* Tap an image to see a zoomable popup
* English and German are fetched simultaneously to provide fallback for missing translations (ETH)
* Swipe right on a mensa to favorite or hide it
* Better use of space on bigger displays
* Improved animations
* More bugfixes and performance improvements
